Application Domain back to ToC

The domain for our application is education and economic status in Ireland.
We have aggregated data such as education level per county, earnings per county, and the number of students per county.
We chose this domain as we are trying to discover if there is an obvious relationship between the level of education in a particular county and its overall economic status.
Are they directly correlated ? Does education = wealth ? Are there more external factors involved ?

            PREFIX rdf: 
            PREFIX xsd: 
            select (?c as ?CountyName) (?mean as ?MeanEarning) 
            (?bachelordegree_value as ?BachelorDegreeHolders)
            where {
                ?s rdf:type  .
                ?s  "2018" .
                ?s  ?c .
                ?s  ?mean .
                
                ?bachelordegree  ?c .
                ?bachelordegree  "2016" .
                ?bachelordegree  "Ordinary bachelor degree/professional qualification or both" .
                ?bachelordegree  "Both sexes".
                ?bachelordegree  ?bachelordegree_value
            }
            ORDER BY DESC(xsd:integer(?bachelordegree_value))
            limit 10

Q2. What county has the lowest number of third level degrees and what age on average does their education cease ? (Merges dataset EZ073 & EA033)

            PREFIX rdf: 
            PREFIX xsd: 
            select  (?c as ?CountyName) (?ageeduceased_value as ?AgeEducationCeased)  
            (SUM(xsd:integer(?degree_value)) as ?DegreeHolders) where {
                ?ageeduceased  ?c .
                ?ageeduceased  "2016" .
                ?ageeduceased  "Average_Age_Education_Ceased_(Number)".
                ?ageeduceased  ?ageeduceased_value .
                
                ?bachelordegree  ?c .
                ?bachelordegree  "2016" .
                
                ?bachelordegree    ?degree.
                        
                FILTER (
                        ?degree in ("Ordinary bachelor degree/professional qualification or both",
                        "Honours bachelor degree/professional qualification or both",
                        "Postgraduate diploma or degree","Doctorate (Ph.D.)")
                )
                
                ?bachelordegree  "Both sexes".
                ?bachelordegree  ?degree_value .
                ?bachelordegree  "Total persons" .
            }
            GROUP BY ?c ?mean ?ageeduceased_value
            ORDER BY ASC(?degreeHolder_sum)
            limit 10

            (?secSchoolValue as ?NumberOfSecondarySchools) (?averageEduEndValue as ?AgeEducationCeased) 
            where {
                ?s rdf:type  .
                ?s  "2018" .
                ?s  ?c .
                ?s  ?mean .
                
                ?secSchool  ?c .
                ?secSchool  "2016" .
                ?secSchool  "Secondary schools" .
                ?secSchool  "Schools providing Second Level Education  (Number)" .
                ?secSchool  ?secSchoolValue .
                
                ?averageEduEnd  ?c .

                ?bachelordegree  ?c .
                ?bachelordegree  "2016" .
